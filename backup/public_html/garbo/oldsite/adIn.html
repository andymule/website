An abstract dystopia. The phases of the ever-evolving chain of being.
An unstable world within worlds demonstrating the connectivity, the
conflict, and the desire of a must that is imbued within every creature.

This desire drives the individual to seek family, and the family to seek
the stability of society. Lines are blurred between audience and performer,
immersing all in possibility. Light, sound, and movement are created
through interactive technologies.

Enveloped in the void, the ringing sound of silence vibrates in space. A
single sound is heard. Light pierces through black. We are born, we exist,
we die – there is no escape. Metamorphosis is the only constant. It creates
as it destroys. We struggle with the change upon which life rests.

Ad Infinitum.
