OTHER WORKS - [the svetlanas]
partly cloudy // sonic iguana

 

BACK
'The Svetlanas' are an Italian punk rock band who play under the guise
of being ex-soviet russian spies. They did a two-week recording session
at Sonic Iguana Studios towards the end of 2009.

For this song, I set up the drum mics, tuned the drum heads, helped
track all instruments, edited all instruments, and played some extra
guitar parts to thicken up the mix. All other work was done by Ryan
Hopper or Mass Giorgini, with Mass making the final masters.
