DESIGN WORK - [hedda gabler]
[top girls] [new kid] [dance] [madagascar]

 

Hedda Gabler is a story about control, both over self and over others, and
how the two relate. We used music during the scene changes to accentuate
the changing moods of Hedda herself. There are times when the actors play
the piano live on stage, one time directly following a scene change. This led
us to re-record all of our pulled music on that same piano to match tonality
and spatial qualities. Here are two of those recordings.
