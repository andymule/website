OTHER WORKS - [partly cloudy]
partly cloudy // sonic iguana


This video is the culmination of my Audio for Video Post Production
class. I designed all of the audio in this piece. I made all of the woosh
sounds by rubbing different fabrics together and performing this live
to video. I did the same for many of the flapping sounds, this time
actually flapping different fabrics in front of a microphone. I am the
voice of the bird in this piece, and my friend, Cole Steinberger, is the
voice of the cloud. I remade the Michael Giacchino's original score as
a MIDI arrangment using East/West Quantum Leap as my orchestral
synth. This was originally mixed for 5.1 surround.
