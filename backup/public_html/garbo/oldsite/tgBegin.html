THEATRE DESIGN - [top girls]
top girls // new kid // dance // madagascar

 

BACK
The entire play was set in the early 1980s except the first scene. In it,
we see a smörgåsbord of women from different eras conversing with
each other, suggesting that this scene takes place in some world that
escapes time. We wanted underscoring music to capture the surreality
of that idea, lending to our dreamy textures. The characters are in a
restaurant, so we also wanted some 'general music' to help establish a
sort of relaxed environment. Finally, we made subtle transitions into
more emotional underscores during monologues addressing the issues
of success and sacrifice, drawing the audience in at key moments.
