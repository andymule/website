THEATRE DESIGN - [top girls]
top girls // new kid // dance // madagascar

 

BACK
The play ends as a child discovers that her aunt is actually her
mother. We used music to accentuate the emotional impact of
that moment by underscoring the final dialogue and growing into
a lighting fade. That cross-faded into a non-80s, non-surreal
curtain call piece to kick the audience back into the real world.



All music in this section was written, performed, and recorded by myself.
