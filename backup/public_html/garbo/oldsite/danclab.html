THEATRE DESIGN - [dance]
top girls // new kid // dance // madagascar

 

BACK
After hearing my dance sound design from the previous fall, one of
Purdue's dance faculty contracted me to work with her on this piece.
The dance was an exploration into fabrics as a metaphor for the
burdens that we carry in our lives. I layered in bothersome textures
for the darker sections to push the audience towards our desired
emotional states. All of the burdensome sections have overbearing
bass rumbles, and I put in bee hives for the toulle section and balloon
stretches for the elastic section for heightened levels of discomfort.

The toulle, elastic, and silk sections are original compositions.

Compliments to Oge Agulué for allowing me use of his amazing voice.
