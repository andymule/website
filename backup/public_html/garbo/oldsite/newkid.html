THEATRE DESIGN - [brand new kid]
top girls // new kid // dance // madagascar

 

The Brand New Kid is a children's musical about embracing our differences.
This music underscored a pivotal dreaming scene.


I faced a unique challenge with this piece, in that I had to
seamlessly transition from the precomposed, provided
music into my dreamlike underscoring. I achieved this
by selecting instruments from a similar palette, but I
added delays, reverbs, and wind chimes to get a
sense of being in an ethereal dream world.

I wrote, performed and recorded this piece myself.
The rest of the musical credits (before 0:09) belong
to Michael Friedman.
